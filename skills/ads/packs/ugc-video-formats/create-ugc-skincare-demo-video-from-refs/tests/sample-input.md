# Sample Input

## Brief
A 15-second intimate bathroom skincare-application UGC video. A woman in her
early twenties is obsessed with a new glow serum — she leans into the camera,
holds the bottle up so the label catches the window light, applies the serum to
her face, turns to show the glow, and holds the bottle up at the end. Warm,
excited, "I'm obsessed" energy. Reads like a real girl filming a quick clip for
friends — NOT a mirror selfie, never a phone in frame. No captions, no music.

## References (to normalize in Phase 1, then bind as @ImageN)
- **Avatar (@Image1)** → woman in her early twenties, brown shoulder-length
  hair, casual cream top, neutral light-grey studio background, empty hands.
- **Product (@Image2)** → "LUMÉ" glow serum: frosted pale-peach glass dropper
  bottle, matte black dropper cap, label reading "LUMÉ / glow serum", white BG.

The bathroom (white tiles, window daylight, towel, plant) comes from the prompt
TEXT — no environment plate.

## Options
- duration: 15 (default)
- resolution: 720p (iteration) / 1080p (delivery)
- aspect_ratio: 9:16

## Expected dialogue (≤ ~28 words total; mouth-visible beats only; application beat silent)
1. (extreme close-up face hook) "Okay, wait—"
2. (product to cheek)           "I cannot believe how good this feels."
3. (fingertip application)      — silent —
4. (glow reveal)                "It just melts in."
5. (final hold)                 "I'm obsessed."

A reference render exists at `demo/finals/lume-skincare-ugc-15s.mp4` for
comparison.
