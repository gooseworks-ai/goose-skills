# Sample Input

## Brief
A 15-second selfie-style UGC product review for a tennis racket. A young female
player shows the racket to a friend on an outdoor court — casual, warm, "this
just arrived and I love it" energy. She talks about the color, the balance, and
the grip, then holds it up as the hero. No captions.

## References (to normalize in Phase 1, then bind as @ImageN)
- **Product** → tennis racket "AURA 300": mint-green-to-white gradient frame,
  vivid orange cross-string grid over white mains, white grip, mint butt cap.
- **Avatar** → female tennis player in a bright lime-green athletic outfit.
- **Environment** (optional) → outdoor blue/green hard court, net, trees, sky.

## Options
- duration: 15 (default)
- resolution: 720p (iteration) / 1080p (delivery)
- aspect_ratio: 9:16

## Expected dialogue (≤ ~28 words total, front-cam beats only)
1. "Okay, it just arrived — I'm obsessed with this color."
2. "It feels balanced, honestly not heavy."
3. "The grip feels really clean."
4. "Yeah, this is the one."
