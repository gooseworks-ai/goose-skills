# Sample Input

## Brief
A 15-second car-confessional UGC video. A guy sitting in his parked car talks
straight to camera in a calm, confiding "let me tell you something" tone about a
small, relatable moment of peace. Testimonial style, no product. No captions.

## Phase 0 answers (this sample exercises the defaults)
- **Avatar** → default demo persona (early-30s man; `demo/assets/refs/avatar.png`).
- **Location/scene** → default scripted parked-car setting.
- **Product** → none (testimonial).

(A product build would instead supply a clean product image and ask the creator
to hold + show it once mid-monologue.)

## Options
- duration: 15 (default)
- resolution: 720p (iteration probe) / 1080p (hero)
- aspect_ratio: 9:16

## Expected monologue (~32–37 words for calm car energy; continuous take, no beats)
"Okay, I have to admit something. I've been parked in this lot for twenty minutes.
Not working. Not driving home. Just sitting here in the quiet. And this? This is
the best part of my day."

A reference render exists at `demo/finals/car-confessional-15s.mp4` for comparison.
