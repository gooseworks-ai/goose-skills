# Sample Input

## Brief
A 15-second selfie-style UGC **walk-and-talk** tips video. A young woman films
herself on her phone at arm's length while **walking down a sunny neighborhood
sidewalk**, talking to camera like she's catching up with a friend. Topic:
"screen-free morning walk" — she stopped grabbing her phone the second she wakes
up and just walks first. **Upbeat, brisk, day-in-the-life energy.** No product.
No captions, no music.

## Phase 0 choices
- **Avatar** → default demo persona (`demo/assets/refs/avatar.png`): warm
  medium-brown skin, dark shoulder-length natural curls worn half-up with loose
  face-framing strands, thin gold hoops + a delicate gold pendant, rust-orange
  ribbed short-sleeve henley, high-waisted light-wash jeans.
- **Location** → default outdoor walkable block: tree-lined sidewalk with
  storefronts, a few (not dense) blurred pedestrians, golden late-morning
  daylight. Rendered from text — no environment plate.
- **Product** → none (testimonial).

## Options
- duration: 15 (default)
- resolution: 720p (probe) / 1080p (hero)
- aspect_ratio: 9:16

## Expected dialogue (~40–45 words, brisk walk energy)
"Nobody tells you this one. I stopped grabbing my phone the second I wake up.
Now I just walk first. No screen, no scroll, no noise. My head's clearer, my
mood's better, I actually sleep now. Try one screen-free walk tomorrow."

A reference render exists at `demo/finals/walk-and-talk-15s.mp4` for comparison.
