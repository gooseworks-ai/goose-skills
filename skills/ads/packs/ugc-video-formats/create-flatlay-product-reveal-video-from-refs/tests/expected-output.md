# Expected Output

## Artifacts
- `master-final.mp4` — 1080×1920, ≈14–16s, **h264** (+ aac music). N beats (sped to
  ~2.67s each) + optional greeting-card insert + a brand end card.
- `generated/flat/*.png` — the top-down flat covers.
- `generated/start/*.png` — the tabletop start frames (flat cover on the plate).
- `generated/beats/*.mp4` — the ~4s Veo hands-cup-and-lift beats.
- `generated/end-card.mp4` — the deterministic HTML end card.
- A poster still (a held-product frame).

## Visual shape (per beat)
- Top-down 90° view of a dressed linen tabletop; one product lies flat, centered.
- Two adult hands enter from the bottom corners, cup the product, hold it centered
  ~1.5s (cover art/text/logo fully readable), then lift and carry it off the bottom.
- Hard cut to the next product. Camera never moves.

## Close + audio
- Optional greeting-card insert (illustration + a handwritten line, fade in/hold/out).
- Brand end card (wordmark + trust icons + CTA pill), ~3s static dwell.
- A warm instrumental music bed, energetic from t=0, fading the tail. No VO, no captions.

## Non-goals
- No talking head, no dialogue, no captions.
- No AI-redesigned covers — the real product art/text/names/logo are preserved exactly.
- No dissolves — hard cuts only. No camera movement within a beat.
