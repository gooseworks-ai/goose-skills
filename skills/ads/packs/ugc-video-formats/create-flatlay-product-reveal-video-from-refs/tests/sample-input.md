# Sample Input

## Brief
A Father's Day "book swap" flat-lay reveal for **Wonderbly** — four personalized
children's books shown one per beat on a dressed linen tabletop; two hands cup each
book, hold it readable, then lift it away; hard-cut between books; a "happy Father's
Day" greeting-card insert; a Wonderbly end card; warm music. Vertical 9:16, ~16s.

## Assets (git-LFS — fetch+checkout first)
- tabletop plate: `assets/tabletop-bg.png` (cream linen + eucalyptus/olive + watches)
- covers (real 3D cover art, transformed to flat top-down per beat):
  `assets/covers/{our-first-fathers-day,daddys-magic-hugs,where-are-you-dad,grandpa-daddy-clover}.png`
- end card: `assets/end-card.html` (Wonderbly wordmark + trust icons + CTA pill)

## Beats (one book each)
1. Our First Father's Day
2. Daddy's Magic Hugs
3. Where Are You? Daddy Edition
4. Grandpa, Daddy and Clover

## Insert + music
- greeting-card insert: "happy Father's Day" (Brush Script) on a soft storybook illo
- music: lively warm acoustic ~130 BPM, festive, instrumental, energetic from t=0

Full config: `scripts/config.example.json`. Reference render:
`demo/finals/wonderbly-fathers-day-reveal.mp4`.
