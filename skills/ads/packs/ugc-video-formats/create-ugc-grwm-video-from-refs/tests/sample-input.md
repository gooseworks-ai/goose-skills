# Sample Input

## Brief
A 15-second "Get Ready With Me" fashion-vlog UGC video. A stylish woman in her
aesthetic bedroom shows off an outfit, playful confident energy — "is this top
crazy or…", reacts to something off-camera, steps to the mirror for the full
look, then a final pose. Single person. No captions.

## References (to normalize in Phase 1, then bind as @ImageN)
- **Avatar** (`@Image1`) → a stylish woman, full-length, plain neutral grey
  basics, plain light-grey studio background, empty hands.
- **Garment** (`@Image2`) → a white short-sleeve tee covered in a scattered
  pattern of bold red five-point stars (clean white-BG product shot).
- **Environment** (`@Image3`, optional but recommended) → aesthetic bedroom:
  full-length mirror, clothing rack, unmade bed, window with soft daylight.

## Options
- duration: 15 (default)
- resolution: 720p (iteration) / 1080p (delivery)
- aspect_ratio: 9:16

## Expected dialogue (≤ ~28 words total, front-cam beats only)
1. "Okay I'm getting ready — is this top crazy or…"
2. "Nope— not taking comments."   (single-person off-camera reaction beat)
3. "Anyway… I kinda love it."     (full-length mirror reveal)
4. "It's chaotic, but it works. I'm wearing this."

A reference render exists at `demo/finals/grwm-stars-15s.mp4` for comparison.
