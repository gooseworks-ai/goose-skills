# Human Test

Run the full skill against `tests/sample-input.md` (or a real brief + avatar
ref) and judge the delivered video by eye. It is a single continuous take — one
person, no cuts.

## Procedure
1. Provide a brief + an avatar reference (default demo persona is fine).
2. Approve the Phase 1 (GPT-image-2 composed sidewalk still) and Phase 3
   (Seedance) prompts at the gates.
3. Let the review-loop report issues; or point out a window that looks wrong.
4. Approve a fix re-render; confirm the stitch lands the new window in place and
   the master audio stays in sync.

## Acceptance criteria (judge the final mp4)
- [ ] Reads as an authentic stranger who stopped to share a hot-take, not a spec ad.
- [ ] **Exactly one person the whole take** — no second person, no interviewer,
      no one else speaking; passersby stay blurred and distant.
- [ ] The creator is **stopped/planted** (not walking, not tripod-locked) with
      believable handheld drift and real micro-motion (gestures, weight shift, a
      glance to the side).
- [ ] Dialogue is intelligible and lip-sync is believable; delivery is punchy.
- [ ] Identity + wardrobe are the SAME start to finish (no morph).
- [ ] Background signage stays blank/illegible — no readable logos or brand text.
- [ ] Duration matches the request; no captions burned in; no music.
- [ ] Every paid call was shown and approved before it fired.

## Red flags (fail)
- A second person, interviewer, or second speaker appears anywhere.
- The creator walks, or the shot reads as a locked tripod.
- A re-render was fired without approval, or captions/music were added.
- The fix replaced a window that itself drifts, or audio drifts after stitching.
