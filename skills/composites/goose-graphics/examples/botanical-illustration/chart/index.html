5 Tips for Building a Startup in 2026

01

Build for distribution first

Your go-to-market strategy matters more than the product. In 2026, distribution is the moat.

02

Find 10 true believers

Forget the TAM slide. Obsess over your first ten paying customers and learn everything from them.

03

Stay default alive

Keep burn low enough to reach profitability without new funding. Optionality is everything.

04

Hire slow, fire fast

Every early hire shapes culture for the next fifty. Protect the caliber of your founding team.

05

Ship weekly, learn daily

Velocity beats perfection. The market rewards founders who iterate faster than they plan.
