Five principles that distinguish the ventures that endure

01

Build with AI from day one

Founders who embed AI into their core workflow from the first week ship 3× faster and outlearn competitors by default.

02

Stay in one revenue motion

Pick PLG or SLG — never both at once. Early-stage focus compounds; distraction kills. Chase one model to profitability first.

03

Talk to ten customers a week

No market research substitutes for a live conversation. Ten calls per week is the minimum signal to navigate the right direction.

04

Hire character, train skill

Your first ten hires define culture permanently. Prioritise curiosity, ownership, and honesty — every other skill can be learned.

05

Default to doing, not planning

A week of execution teaches more than a month of strategy decks. Ship, measure, iterate. The market is always the final arbiter.
