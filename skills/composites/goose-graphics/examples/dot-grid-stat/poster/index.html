5

ESSENTIAL TIPS

for building a startup in 2026

Distilled from founders who shipped, pivoted, and survived the current AI wave.

01

Ship fast, learn faster

Launch an MVP in weeks, not months. Speed of iteration beats perfect planning every time. Embarrass yourself early.

02

Build AI into the core, not the periphery

In 2026, AI is infrastructure. Embed it from day one — retrofitting later costs 3x more in time and technical debt.

03

Hire for leverage, not headcount

Small teams with high-leverage tooling consistently outperform large bloated orgs. One 10x hire beats three average ones.

04

Obsess over 10 users before 10,000

Deep retention with a few beats shallow acquisition at scale. Talk to your users daily until you can't stand not to.

05

Revenue beats runway

Default to revenue-positive as early as possible. Fundraising is a tool, not a strategy. Charge people money.
