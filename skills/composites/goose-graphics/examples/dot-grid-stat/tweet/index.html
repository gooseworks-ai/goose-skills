5 tips for building a startup in 2026:
1. Ship before you're ready. Your users will tell you what's broken faster than any spec doc.
2. Distribution beats product. A mediocre product with great reach wins every time.
3. Hire for execution, not credentials. The best early hires ship things, not decks.
4. Default to async. Every sync hour is expensive when time compounds.
5. Revenue is the only metric that matters in year one. Everything else is a leading indicator at best.
Save this for when you're second-guessing yourself.
