5 tips for building a startup in 2026 🧵
1. Ship before you're ready. Your first 100 users will teach you more than 100 planning sessions ever will.
2. Build in public. Trust compounds faster than stealth. Let the world watch you solve the problem.
3. Use AI as leverage, not a replacement. The best founders in 2026 amplify themselves with agents.
4. Revenue > funding. Default alive from day one. Investors follow traction, not pitch decks.
5. Hire for momentum, not resume. Energy is the real unfair advantage in an early team.
