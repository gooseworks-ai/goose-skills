5 tips for building a startup in 2026

01

Ship before you're ready. The market will tell you more in a week than six months of planning ever could.

02

Distribution beats product. A mediocre product with great distribution beats a great product nobody finds.

03

Hire for hunger, not credentials. The best builders I know don't have MBAs — they have scars.

04

Revenue is your runway. Don't optimize for raises; optimize for customers who pay.

05

Protect your time ruthlessly. Every meeting that could've been an email is a feature that doesn't exist yet.
