5 tips for building a startup in 2026 that no one tells you:

1. Build in public. Distribution is the moat, not the product.

2. Use AI to go from 1 to 10 before you start hiring — not after.

3. Your first 10 customers are your real co-founders. Talk to them every single day.

4. Revenue > virality. Always. Impressions don't pay your team.

5. If v1 doesn't embarrass you, you shipped too late.

Learned all of this the hard way across 3 companies.
