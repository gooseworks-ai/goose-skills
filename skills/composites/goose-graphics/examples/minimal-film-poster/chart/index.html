5 Tips for
Building a Startup

01

Build for pain, not passion

Solve a real problem people already pay to fix. Passion follows traction.

02

Talk to 10 users every week

Your assumptions are wrong until real users prove them right.

03

Default to action

Imperfect and shipped beats perfect and planned. Always.

04

Revenue beats vanity metrics

If no one is paying, you have a project — not a business.

05

Hire slow, fire fast

One wrong hire costs more than months of an empty seat.
